Detecting emotions for HRI

Using the SEWA dataset to measure the arousal and valence for continuous emotions: amusement, empathy, liking and boredom.

Aim to use a combination of audio and visual transcripts to detect these emotions.

This will be done by implementing a training network of neurons to train for the verbal and audio cues.

Pytorch is the library that I will be using. This research will be implemented in robots for HRI.
